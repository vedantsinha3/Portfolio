About Me

Hey there! I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student at UC Merced who can't resist a good coding challenge or the thrill of exploring new tech frontiers. What started with a simple website experiment has blossomed into a full-blown passion for AI, Computer Vision, and innovative web solutions.

I love hanging out where creativity and logic collide turning knotty problems into smooth, user-friendly experiences. Whether I'm building responsive web apps, or sketching out mobile interfaces, my guiding principle is simple: technology should empower and delight.

When I'm not immersed in code, you'll usually find me hunting for the next scoop of ice cream, wandering through art galleries, or diving into the latest research papers (and maybe even contributing to an open-source project or two). I'm a lifelong learner at heart, always eager to share what I've picked up with fellow students and stay one step ahead in our ever-evolving digital world.
